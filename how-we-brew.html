How We Brew

Four ingredients.
No compromises.

We start with the finest quality malt, hops, water, and yeast to create our base beer. Using traditional brewing methods combined with advanced fermentation techniques, we craft a flavorful beer and then gently remove the alcohol while preserving the complex flavors and aromas that make beer special. No corner cutting, ever.

Beyond the Buzz.

Non-alcoholic beer doesn't mean inferior beer. At Kit, we've mastered the art of removing alcohol while preserving all the flavor complexity, aroma, and satisfaction you expect from a premium craft beer. Every batch is crafted with the same dedication and attention to detail as traditional craft beers. Your choice to drink non-alcoholic should never be a compromise.
