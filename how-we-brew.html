HOW WE BREW

Four Ingredients. No Compromises.

Our Brewing Process

We start with the finest quality malt, hops, water, and yeast to create our base beer. Using traditional brewing methods combined with advanced fermentation techniques, we craft a flavorful beer and then gently remove the alcohol while preserving the complex flavors and aromas that make beer special.

Our process ensures that every bottle of KIT delivers authentic craft beer taste without any compromise. We never cut corners on quality or flavor, which is why KIT stands out in the non-alcoholic beer market.

Four Essential Ingredients

Water

Pure, clean water is the foundation of every great beer, making up the majority of our product.

Malt

Premium malt grains provide the complex flavors, aromas, and body that define our beer's character.

Hops

Hops contribute bitterness, aroma, and flavor. We carefully select varieties to create our distinctive taste profiles.

Yeast

Our specially selected yeast strains ferment our beer to perfection, creating the flavors you love.

Beyond the Buzz

Non-alcoholic beer doesn't mean inferior beer. At KIT, we've mastered the art of removing alcohol while preserving all the flavor complexity, aroma, and satisfaction you expect from a premium craft beer. Our brewing process respects the beer itself, ensuring that what ends up in your glass is nothing short of exceptional.

Every batch is crafted with the same dedication and attention to detail as traditional craft beers. We believe that your choice to drink non-alcoholic should never be a compromise on quality, taste, or the overall beer experience.
