OUR STORY

Under Your Own Influence.

We're Here For People Who Don't Need a Drink to Belong — They Need a Drink That Belongs to Them.

KIT isn't just about what's missing from the can — it's about what's inside: bold flavor, exceptional craftsmanship, and a choice that aligns effortlessly with a life of intention. For the health-conscious and socially active, for parents juggling family life, or professionals chasing balance, KIT is crafted to enhance every moment — on your own terms.

We get it — life is busy, full, and worth showing up for. Whether it's deep conversation around the fire, cheering at the game, or a family dinner that starts with chaos and ends with laughter, KIT keeps you fully in the moment. No compromises, no side effects, just the satisfaction of a great beer and the confidence of being exactly where you want to be. Because when you're Under Your Own Influence, you don't miss a thing. And neither should your beer.

Meet the Founders

William Fisher and Rob Barrett are the co-founders of KIT Non-Alc, based in Portland, Maine. Will brings deep craft beer expertise to KIT, having co-founded Austin Street Brewery in 2012 — one of the pre-eminent craft breweries in Portland, ME — before turning his attention to reimagining what non-alcoholic beer could be.

Together, Will and Rob set out to prove that choosing non-alcoholic doesn't mean settling for less. Drawing on years of brewing experience and a shared belief that great beer should be for everyone, they built KIT from the ground up — with real craft credentials and an uncompromising commitment to flavor.

Your Kind of Craft

Every batch of KIT is brewed with the same passion and precision as the finest craft beers, using the highest quality ingredients and time-tested brewing techniques. We believe that the non-alcoholic beer experience should be just as rich, complex, and satisfying as any craft beer on the shelf.
