OUR STORY

Tomorrow's Favorite Beer.

Real Beer. Real People.

Rob Barrett and Will Fisher founded KIT Non-Alc Brewing in 2021 in Portland, Maine, building on the heritage of Austin Street Brewery. Our mission was simple: create authentic, flavorful non-alcoholic beer that doesn't compromise on taste or quality.

We believe that choosing a non-alcoholic beer shouldn't mean settling for less. Every batch of KIT is crafted with the same passion and precision as the finest craft beers, using the highest quality ingredients and time-tested brewing techniques.

Sound Mind Live Partnership

KIT is proud to partner with Sound Mind Live, an organization dedicated to mental health awareness and support. We believe that enjoying a great beer should go hand-in-hand with taking care of your mental health and being fully present with those around you.

A portion of proceeds from select KIT products support Sound Mind Live initiatives and mental health advocacy programs.

Sustainability Commitment

We're committed to brewing responsibly. KIT uses sustainable sourcing practices for our ingredients, minimizes waste in our production process, and works to reduce our environmental impact at every step of the brewing journey.
