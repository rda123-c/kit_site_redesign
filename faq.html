Product Questions

Is there alcohol in your non-alcoholic beer?

All KIT beers contain less than 0.5% ABV (alcohol by volume), which classifies them as non-alcoholic according to industry standards. This minimal alcohol content comes naturally from the fermentation process.

Are any of your beers gluten free?

Our beers are gluten removed, not gluten free. We use a special process to remove gluten from our malt, making KIT suitable for most people with gluten sensitivity. However, if you have celiac disease or severe gluten allergy, please consult with your doctor before consuming.

What are the ingredients?

KIT Non-Alcoholic Beer is brewed with just four essential ingredients:

  • Water
  • Malt
  • Hops
  • Yeast

No artificial flavors, colors, or additives.

What are the nutrition facts?

Per 12 FL OZ serving:

  • Calories: 86
  • Carbs: 18g
  • Protein: 1.5g
  • Fat: 0g
What are the IBU's for each flavor?

IBU (International Bitterness Units) varies by style. Visit our product page for detailed specifications for each flavor variant.

Is non-alcoholic beer safe for pregnant women?

While KIT contains less than 0.5% ABV, we recommend consulting with your doctor before consuming any alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverages during pregnancy.

Shipping & Orders

Where do you ship?

We ship nationwide across the United States. Due to shipping regulations, we are currently unable to ship to certain states. Please check our shipping policy for complete information.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days from the date of order. Transit times may vary depending on your location and current shipping volume.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders of 4+ six-packs. This helps us reduce packaging waste and get great beer to you affordably.

I want to buy beer as a gift - how long will it last?

KIT Non-Alcoholic Beer is best enjoyed fresh. Check the best-by date on each bottle. When stored in a cool, dark place, our beer maintains optimal flavor for several months after purchase.
